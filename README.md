# iGaming Analytics Dashboard (Sportsbook & Casino Performance)

This project analyzes iGaming platform data across multiple countries to evaluate customer behavior, revenue performance, and product trends across Sportsbook and Casino segments over time.

## 🎯 Objective
- Analyze revenue performance across different countries  
- Compare Sportsbook vs Casino contribution to total revenue  
- Understand customer conversion from sign-ups to active users  
- Evaluate the relationship between deposits and revenue generation  

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Excel (Data Cleaning & Preparation)
- Tableau (Interactive Dashboard)
- PowerPoint (Business Presentation)

## 📊 Key Insights
- Revenue varies significantly across countries, indicating strong regional performance differences  
- Casino and Sportsbook contribute differently to total revenue depending on the market  
- Higher deposits generally correlate with higher revenue, though inconsistencies exist across regions  
- Increased customer activity does not always lead to higher revenue, highlighting differences in customer value  
- Some regions show lower revenue despite user growth, suggesting potential inefficiencies or lower monetization  

## 💼 Business Value
- Identifies high-performing and underperforming markets  
- Helps understand product performance (Sportsbook vs Casino)  
- Supports data-driven decisions for marketing and customer targeting  
- Provides insights into revenue drivers and customer engagement  

## 📁 Files Included
- Raw and cleaned dataset (country + time-based data)  
- Tableau dashboard (.twbx)  
- Presentation slides
